
##聚集符##

+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------+
| 参数名                                                                   | 描述                                                                     | 示例                                           |
+==========================================================================+==========================================================================+================================================+
| [$project](SdbDoc_Cn/reference/operator/aggregate_operator/project.html) | 选择需要输出的字段名，"1"表示输出，"0"表示不输出，还可以实现字段的重命名 | {$project:{field1:!,field:0,aliase:"$field3"}} |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------+
| [$match](SdbDoc_Cn/reference/operator/aggregate_operator/match.html)     | 实现从集合中选择匹配条件的记录，相当于SQL语句的where                     | {$match:{field:{$lte:value}}}                  |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------+
| [$limit](SdbDoc_Cn/reference/operator/aggregate_operator/limit.html)     | 限制返回的记录条数                                                       | {$limit:10}                                    |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------+
| [$skip](SdbDoc_Cn/reference/operator/aggregate_operator/skip.html)       | 控制结果集的开始点，即跳过结果集中指定条数的记录                         | {$skip:5}                                      |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------+
| [$group](SdbDoc_Cn/reference/operator/aggregate_operator/group.html)     | 实现对记录的分组，类似于SQL的group by语句，"_id"指定分组字段             | {$group:{_id:"$field"}}                        |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------+
| [$sort](SdbDoc_Cn/reference/operator/aggregate_operator/sort.html)       | 实现对结果集的排序，"1"代表升序，"-1"代表降序                            | {$sort:{field1:1,field2:-1,...}}               |
+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+--------------------------------------------------------------------------+------------------------------------------------+
